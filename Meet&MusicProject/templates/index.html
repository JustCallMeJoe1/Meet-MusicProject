Welcome to Music & Meet!


Music & Meet offers a platform to find music events that are occuring in your area. Connect with others by reserving a spot at a local music venue. Music & Meet believes that by offering a platform to users, we can connect like minded music lovers to each other over a music medium! Whether if you're looking for a specfic music event, or just browsing the upcoming music venues, Music & Meet is an excellent platform for you to use!
